About Us

Rubik's3 is meant to create a centralized community for speed cubers! We have created a user friendly interface that allows any member to create a competition. Each competition undergoes review by WCA delegates, who can contact you to discuss your competition.


Features

Messaging

Talk with your friends, or meet new cubers! This is a fast way to invite your friends to join your newly created competition

WCA Authorized

Log In with your WCA credentials


blog




Create Competitions

When you sign-up/sign-in, you can create as many competitions as you want. All competitions are reviewed by WCA delegates

Manage Competitions

You can customize the competition to include events you want! You can allow other users to volunteer and help organize events.

View Competitions

You can view all the competitions created, and narrow down your search to competitions in your area.
